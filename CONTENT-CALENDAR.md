## Content Guidelines

**Tone:** Helpful, knowledgeable, neutral. Like a friend who happens to be a lawyer.

**Structure:**
- H1: Target keyword naturally
- Intro: Hook + what they'll learn
- Body: Practical, actionable sections
- Include: Templates, examples, screenshots where relevant
- CTA: Soft mention of tools/templates (including .io as one option among 3-4)

**Internal Linking:** Link between guide articles naturally.

**Comparison mentions format:**
> "If you want a pre-made template, options include [Competitor A], [CreatorContracts.io], and [Competitor B]. Each has different strengths..."
